How it Works

How It Works

1. Locate & Onboard

Find Your Local Node. Look out for the official SachetBank Banner at local shops on your street. Walk up to the shop owner and they will instantly register your WhatsApp phone number to activate your digital wallet.

Welcome Gift: We’ll hand you a Mesh Bag completely free to start your sachet waste storing journey at home!

2. Store Clean & Dry

Store only PURE WATER Sachets. Hang your mesh bag in your kitchen. Ensure the sachets are completely empty of water, clean, and dry, then toss it inside.

Quality Matters: Our downstream fashion house, DunsinCrafts, weaves these sachet nylons into high-end luxury art. Because of this, dirty or mixed landfill waste will be rejected!

3. Drop Off & Mint

Weigh and Record Instantly. Once your mesh bag is full, bring it back to the terminal where you registered. The shop owner will weigh it, take a quick photo for ledger verification, and enter the weight into our system.

Instant Receipt: The exact moment the weight is logged, you will receive an automated WhatsApp receipt showing your new wallet balance. 1 KG of pristine waste = 50 Points added directly to your digital wallet.

4. Redeem Rewards

Shop with Your Waste. You can choose to keep increasing your wallet points or use your available balance right there at the counter to unlock essential household food items:

  • Garri (1 Cup): 50 Pts
  • Rice (1 Cup): 220 Pts
  • Beans (1 Cup): 220 Pts
  • Eggs (1 Unit): 250 Pts
  • Noodles (1 Sachet): 250 Pts
  • Palm Oil (1 Cup): 330 Pts
  • Vegetable Oil (1 Cup): 400 Pts

Select Redeem Reward, fill your cart, click Checkout Now, and the shop owner will hand over your fresh groceries instantly. Take your empty mesh bag back home, and repeat the cycle!
