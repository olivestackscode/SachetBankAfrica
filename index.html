Turning Sachet Waste into
Nutritious Food Rewards

We help communities clean their environment by aggregating plastic sachet waste. Deposit your plastic, earn points, and redeem them for food items at local partner terminals.

How it Works
🫙

1. Collect

Store your used plastic sachets in our provided mesh bags.

⚖️

2. Weigh

Take your bag to an authorized aggregator terminal for verification.

🍲

3. Redeem

Instantly earn points and swap them for food rewards!
